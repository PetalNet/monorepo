Point · Client Visual Direction

Bold, monochrome, tactile.

The native Point experience is pure black & white: confident greyscale, strong weight contrast, big soft curves. Every drop of color is held in reserve, so that when bridges arrive, color is what marks an entity as bridged from another network. Below: the real screens, self-contained (fonts embedded, nothing pulled from the web). Pick the type.
