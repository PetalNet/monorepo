05 Motion

Every change, quietly

Every state change animates, fast and subtle enough to read as "nice," not noticeable. Idle stays silent; nothing reveals on scroll. prefers-reduced-motion is a real branch in the code.

Light and dark, an instant cut

The theme swaps instantly, with no cross-fade and no flash. Paint props are suspended for one frame so the tokens flip hard; the reserved scrollbar gutter keeps it jump-free.

Button presses, tactile

A press-scale and a quick accent ink-in from the point you pressed.

Motion tokens

09 Accessibility

Built in, not bolted on

  • Visible focus rings. 2px accent, 2px offset on focus-visible. Tab through to see it.
  • Generous tap targets. At least 32px; 40px for primary actions.
  • Keyboard everywhere. Every control is reachable and operable.
  • Reasonable AA contrast. Text and the theme-aware accent measure to AA on both surfaces. Not AAA everywhere.
  • Reduced motion honored. As a real branch in the code, not just a CSS speed-up.
  • System theme respected. First visit follows the OS; your choice persists after.
